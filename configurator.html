Process

Four steps into a measured quote.

The configurator moves from family direction to submission in a controlled sequence.

01

Select a family

Forma, Aurelia, or Seraphine — the language of the mantel is set here.

02

Refine silhouette

Profile, leg, shelf, and proportion are adjusted within the family's vocabulary.

03

Choose material

Natural stone direction and finish, selected from a curated palette.

04

Submit for review

Scope, dimensions, and project notes are prepared, then sent for a measured quote.

Craft

Natural stone, resolved by hand.

Each mantel is worked from block to finished piece through a disciplined sequence — cutting, carving, and final refinement carried out with attention to the stone's grain and weight.

The configurator reflects this logic: direction, silhouette, and finish chosen in the same order the piece is resolved.
